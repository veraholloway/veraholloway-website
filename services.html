Work With Me

Vera provides the following types of Therapy:

  • Individual Counseling
  • Pre-Marriage and/or Relationship Counseling
  • Marriage & Family Counseling

Types of Therapy

  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) — effective in treating borderline personality disorder, people who are experiencing thoughts of suicide, eating disorders, PTSD, substance use disorders, and mood disorders

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) — effective in treating a variety of disorders, such as anxiety disorders, depression, bipolar disorder, eating disorders, schizophrenia, trauma-related disorders

  • Exposure Therapy — a form of CBT used to treat clients with fear and anxiety disorders, OCD, PTSD, and other phobias

  • Family Systems Therapies — used in helping individuals and families dealing with substance misuse disorders, eating disorders, behavioral problems, anxiety, OCD, or medical issues

Cost of Services

  • Vera is in-network with most major insurance companies and bills her services to the client's medical insurance. Clients are responsible for the co-pay or co-insurance at the time the service is rendered.

  • Vera is in-network with the following insurance companies:

    • Blue Cross Blue Shield (in state and out of state policies)
    • United Health Care
    • UMR
    • United Health Care Community Plan
    • Gilsbar 360

  • If Vera is not in-network with the client's insurance, she will submit an out-of-network claim on behalf of the client; however, the client is responsible for payment in full at each session.

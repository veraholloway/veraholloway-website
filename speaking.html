Hey Y'all!

Over the years, Vera has spent a great deal of time working with women held back by shame, insecurities, brokenness, heartache, rejection, guilt, and unworthiness. During her doctoral study, she began to take a closer look at all these core issues women were experiencing. Combining core women’s issues, her own personal life experiences, and her clients’ experiences, she wrote a book, Beauty From Ashes: My Journey from Broken to Beautiful which was based on the curriculum of the same name.

Whether it’s a business event, workshop, or church conference, Vera loves meeting new people and sharing her knowledge and practical applications for emotional health.

Her calendar fills quickly, so secure your dates today!

About Vera

More than a counselor —
a fellow traveler.

Vera Holloway grew up in the bayou country of south Louisiana, where life moves at the pace of the water and roots run deep. That grounded, unhurried wisdom shapes everything she does — whether she's sitting across from a client, standing on a stage, or penning words meant to heal.

As owner of Holloway Counseling Services and a passionate women's ministry leader, Vera weaves clinical expertise with faith-based compassion to help women find their footing again.

Resources

Whether you are working with Vera personally or interested in what she shares with people she works with, here are a few resources for you

SYMBIS

I am a certified SYMIS Facilitator (SYMBIS stands for Saving Your Marriage Before it Starts)

Clients will take the assessment through a link that is emailed to them and we will break down the results in our sessions. They will leave with lots of information and a detailed report.

https://www.symbis.com/

Enneagram

When working with certain clients I will often have them take the enneagram test. I know they have lots of free versions out there, but I am a believer that you get what you pay for!

The free version will only take you so far. I like to unpack the results with my clients and feel this is the best version of the test!

https://tests.enneagraminstitute.com/

EMDR

I am a trained EMDR therapist (EMDR — stands for Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) — this is a great website that explains more about EMDR.

https://www.emdria.org/about-emdr-therapy/

Christian Counseling

The American Association of Christian Counselors

I am a part of this Christian counseling organization and they put out great information and offer great training opportunities both for professionals and non-professionals.

https://www.aacc.net/
